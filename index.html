About Us

Poonam's Gujarati Bhojnalaya is dedicated to bringing authentic Gujarati cuisine to Chennai. Our restaurant prides itself on using fresh, high-quality ingredients to prepare delicious vegetarian dishes. Founded with a passion for sharing the rich flavors of Gujarati food, we strive to provide a warm and welcoming dining experience for all our guests.
